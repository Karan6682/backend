Meta Permanent Token Setup

Setup a permanent Meta Access Token in 5 minutes. No more token expiry issues, ever.

Step 01

Meta Business Settings

First, open your Meta Business Suite settings in a new tab.

business.facebook.com/settings

Click the box above to copy the URL.

Step 02

Create System User

Go to Users > System Users in left menu.
Click the blue Add button.
Name it CRM_Admin and set role to Admin.
Step 03

Assign App Assets

Select your new CRM_Admin user.
Click Add Assets → select Apps.
Select your WhatsApp App and turn ON Full Control.
Step 04

Generate Permanent Token

Click Generate New Token and select your App.
IMPORTANT: Select Permanent expiration.
Tick: whatsapp_business_management & whatsapp_business_messaging
Step 05

Connect to CRM

Copy the generated token immediately (Meta won't show it again!).
Go back to CloudCRM > Connect Meta.
Paste token and click Start Discovery.
